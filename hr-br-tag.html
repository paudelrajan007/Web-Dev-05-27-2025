Steve Jobs

Born:
February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, USA

Adopted:
Raised by Paul and Clara Jobs

College Dropout:
Left Reed College but kept learning on his own

Co-founded Apple:
In 1976 with Steve Wozniak, created Apple in a garage

What You Can Learn from Him:
Think differently
Focus on quality
Keep things simple
Follow your passion
Take bold risks
Believe in your vision

His Way of Thinking:
User-focused
Perfectionist
Powerful storyteller
Controlled every detail of the product

Famous:

“Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life.”
“Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower.”
